Caring for Your Best Friend

Alpha Vet Clinic is dedicated to providing exceptional veterinary care for pets in Dubai, UAE. Located on 2nd December Street, the clinic offers a wide range of services, from routine check-ups to specialized treatments, all delivered in a state-of-the-art facility. With a team of passionate veterinarians, Alpha Vet Clinic ensures the health and happiness of every pet, supported by 24/7 emergency on-call services, professional grooming, and convenient features like valet parking and pick-and-drop services. Their commitment extends beyond medical care, with active participation in local adoption events, educational workshops, and eco-friendly practices to promote animal welfare and sustainability. Visit alphavetdubai.com to learn more or book an appointment.

Our Role

Design by Ching partnered with Alpha Vet Clinic to deliver a complete Brand Design System in just one week, enabling Dr. Karim Ragab to establish a professional and modern clinic presence in Dubai. Under Mike Ching’s direction, we created a fast-tracked MVP that included clinic signage, staff uniforms, pet passports, and digital assets. Our cohesive identity, featuring a modern logo, vibrant color palette, and tailored typography, reflects Alpha Vet Clinic’s commitment to exceptional pet care and community engagement. The design system was crafted to resonate with pet owners while ensuring scalability for future growth.


Our agile approach prioritized collaboration and efficiency, ensuring Alpha Vet Clinic’s vision was realized with precision. The result is a brand that communicates trust, compassion, and professionalism, supporting their mission to provide top-notch veterinary care. Visit alphavetdubai.com to explore their services and book an appointment.
