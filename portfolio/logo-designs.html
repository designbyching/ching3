Logo Designs

Over the years, Mike Ching has created a wide range of professional logos and brand identities for clients across different industries. This collection showcases a selection of logo designs developed between 2009 and 2025. In most cases, these projects extended well beyond just logo creation — including full brand strategy, visual identity systems, typography selection, color palettes, business collateral, and digital applications.

Each mark was crafted with attention to meaning, scalability, memorability, and timeless appeal while aligning with the client's personality, values, and market positioning.
