Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Design by Ching offer?

We provide a full spectrum of services, including logo design, branding, UI/UX design, web development, and digital transformation, tailored to your business needs.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A logo design may take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive digital projects can span 2-6 months. We’ll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! While based in Sydney, we serve clients worldwide, leveraging remote collaboration tools to ensure seamless communication and delivery.

What tools do you use for design and development?

Our full-stack team uses industry-leading tools like Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, React, Node.js, and WordPress, ensuring cutting-edge solutions for every project.
